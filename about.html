About CogniSol | CFMS

A boutique consultancy and technology firm delivering finance, management, and custom software solutions with a hands-on, owner-led approach.

Who We Are

CogniSol | CFMS is a sole proprietorship consultancy focused on helping organizations improve performance through a unique blend of financial advisory, management consulting, and custom technology solutions.

Integrated Consulting & Technology

Unlike traditional IT vendors or pure consulting firms, we combine strategic advisory with hands-on system development. This ensures that business recommendations are translated into practical, working systems.

Owner-Led Delivery Model

As a sole proprietorship, engagements are led directly by the principal consultant. This ensures accountability, continuity, and deep understanding of each client’s business context.

Business-First Technology

Technology is applied as a business enabler — not as an end in itself. Solutions are designed around operational realities, financial controls, compliance requirements, and management decision-making.

Consulting Domains

Our consulting practice spans three core domains, allowing us to address business challenges holistically.

Finance Consulting

Advisory focused on financial controls, reporting structures, cost optimization, working capital management, and management information systems (MIS).

Management Consulting

Process improvement, operational efficiency, governance frameworks, KPI design, and organizational workflow optimization to improve execution and accountability.

Software & Systems Consulting

System architecture, automation strategy, data platforms, compliance systems, and custom application design aligned with business strategy.

Our Philosophy

We believe sustainable business improvement requires alignment between strategy, processes, and systems.

Practical Over Theoretical

Recommendations are grounded in real operational constraints and implemented through working systems, not just reports.

Long-Term Partnership

We aim to become a trusted long-term partner, supporting continuous improvement rather than one-time project delivery.

Measured Outcomes

Focus on measurable business outcomes such as efficiency gains, improved controls, better visibility, and reduced operational risk.

Our Vision & Commitment to Women Empowerment

At CogniSol | CFMS, our vision extends beyond business performance to creating inclusive opportunities through technology and consulting.

Inclusive Workforce Development

We are committed to creating opportunities for women to participate meaningfully in finance, management, and technology roles. This includes flexible work models, skill development, and mentorship-focused engagement to support long-term career growth.

Women-Led Business Enablement

We actively support women entrepreneurs and women-led enterprises by providing accessible consulting, digital transformation guidance, and customized software tools to strengthen operational and financial foundations.

Technology as an Equalizer

We believe technology can reduce structural barriers by enabling remote work, automation, and digital access to business systems. Our solutions are designed to support greater participation of women in professional and entrepreneurial ecosystems.
